Emotional Health

anxiety

Emotional health refers to the ability to understand, manage, and express emotions in a healthy and balanced way, encompassing self-awareness, resilience, and the capacity to navigate life's challenges.

Tips to Improve:

  • Get enough sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Exercise regularly
  • Practice self-care
  • Connect with others
  • Engage in mindfulness and meditation
  • Be kind to yourself
  • Express your emotions
  • Seek professional help when needed
